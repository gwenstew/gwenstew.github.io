How it Works

CNNs are a type of neural network that use grid-like data to learn features. This is why typically CNNs are used for image analysis because CNNs are great at detecting patterns and extracting features of the image. When I was researching this I was confused how this could be applied to sound waves which are just streams of amplitude samples. This was until I learned that the melspectrogram can be used to classify sound.

Melspectrogram
